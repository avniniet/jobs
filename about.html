Our Goals

At HR Forte, we visualize to bring organizational growth through talent, and at the same time, help job seekers top the career ladder by becoming their launchpad. We want to set a benchmark in the recruitment industry that businesses can look after when they need HR and workforce solutions.

HR Forte’s purpose is to reshape the recruitment landscape that should include three of our key stakeholders: organizations, candidates, and the community we call home. We want to help businesses find the right fit, candidates the right role, and stakeholders the right support.

Our Work

As a total human resource service provider working globally, we understand that people are the cornerstone of a business’s success. No matter what products you offer or how great strategies you develop, you can’t yield results without having the right talent in your team. This understanding has led us to be fully committed to delivering you HR and consulting solutions best-suited to your unique business needs. When you partner with us, you can rest assured that all your staffing and human resource needs will be taken care of. Plus, our goal to ensure the highest professional standards possible enables us to offer cost-effective, prompt, and outcomedriven services to organizations. HR Forte helps both businesses and job seekers reach their goals by acting as a reliable bridge between them.

Besides helping organizations find the right fit for their staff, HR Forte helps candidates find their dream job, fulfilling their career goals. As an HR and workforce solutions company, we not only help job seekers match with the right job opportunity, but we also offer them professional HR guidance from the employer’s perspective to help them kickstart or advance their careers. Our services are free from registration, no matter your profile or professional experience.

HR Forte has helped more than a hundred companies, including a few Fortune 500 companies, SMEs, and others, meet their staffing needs in a short span. This has earned us the reputation and trust among our clients. And we continue to march towards establishing ourselves as a leader in the HR realm that organizations and candidates can trust and work with.

COVID-19 and HR Landscape Across the Nation

The COVID-19 pandemic has taken a drastic toll on almost every industry, and recruitment is no exception. Lockdowns imposed in the wake of the pandemic have not only resulted in the loss of millions of jobs but, at the same, also triggered scarcity of jobs across the globe, as companies put a halt to recruitment. According to a report, India faced an 18% decline in hiring activity in April post lockdown, crippling the recruitment industry. But as the nation recovers from COVID-19, HR Forte aims to work day in and out to support companies and the Indian Economy.
